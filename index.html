The signal path

How a missed call becomes a booked job.

Every call moves through the same precise sequence. You approve the rules once. Nevamis runs them on every ring.

  1. Call comes in

    Your number forwards to Nevamis when you choose: always, after hours, or when you can't pick up.

  2. Answered in your tone

    The AI answers in your business's voice and style, on the first ring.

  3. Caller qualified

    It asks the questions you approved: job type, urgency, location, timing.

  4. Job booked

    It books directly into available slots, following your calendar rules.

  5. Customer confirmed

    The caller gets an SMS confirmation before they can dial your competitor.

  6. You get the summary

    Name, number, need, and outcome land in your pocket after every call.

Straight answers

Questions owners actually ask.

Will callers know it is AI?

Yes. The agent identifies itself naturally, then gets to work. In practice callers care that the phone was answered and the job got booked. You can hear this yourself on the demo line.

Will it sound robotic?

Call (587) 413-0035 and judge. It speaks in a natural voice, handles interruptions, and keeps answers short the way a good receptionist does.

Can I keep my current phone number?

Yes. Your number stays exactly as it is. Call forwarding sends calls to Nevamis always, after hours, or only when you miss the call. You can switch it off from your own phone at any time.

Can it answer only after hours or when we miss a call?

Yes. That conditional coverage is the most common setup: you answer when you can, Nevamis catches everything else instead of voicemail.

Can it book directly into my calendar?

Yes, booking into connected calendar systems is part of the setup. We confirm your exact calendar or job-management tool during the strategy call.

What happens when a caller has an unusual question?

The agent only commits to what your rules allow. For anything outside them it takes a message, flags the summary, or transfers, depending on how we configure escalation with you.

Can it transfer urgent calls?

Yes, live transfer to a number you choose is a configurable rule. We define together what counts as urgent for your business.

Can it handle multiple locations or languages?

Multi-location routing and additional languages are scoped during discovery, since the right setup depends on your call flows. Ask on the strategy call.

How long does setup take?

Days, not months. Discovery, build, testing against your real scenarios, then a controlled pilot on your line.

Can I review calls and summaries?

Yes. Every call produces a summary, and call review is part of how we tune your agent over time.

What does it cost?

Pricing is scoped to your call volume and configuration on a short strategy call, and service is month to month. It is designed to cost a fraction of staffing the phone.

How is customer information handled?

Call details are used to run your service: booking, confirmations, and your summaries. See the privacy page for what this website itself collects. Businesses remain responsible for notices appropriate to their own jurisdiction and call-recording requirements.

What if it is not a fit?

Month to month, cancel anytime, and the 30-day guarantee above. The pilot exists so you can judge from real calls, not promises.
