Privacy

Last updated: July 2026. This page describes what this website collects. It is written to be read, not to hide things.

What this website collects

This website does not use advertising trackers and does not set non-essential cookies. It stores one preference in your browser (your motion on/off choice) which never leaves your device. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which receives standard technical request data (such as your IP address) when the fonts load.

When you call the demo line

Calls to (587) 413-0035 are handled by an AI voice agent running on third-party telephony and voice platforms. Call audio, transcripts, and any details you share (such as your name or callback number) are processed to run the conversation and to follow up with you if you ask us to. Do not share sensitive personal information on the demo line.

When you book a call

Bookings are handled by our scheduling provider (Cal.com). The information you enter there (name, email, and anything you add) is used to run the meeting and follow up. Their own privacy terms apply to their processing.

When you email us

Email to Sales@nevamis.ca is used to reply to you. We do not sell contact information.

For businesses using Nevamis

Businesses deploying a Nevamis agent on their own phone line are responsible for the notices and consent appropriate to their jurisdiction and their call-recording configuration. This page is not legal advice.
