One after-hours call, start to finish.

A local HVAC company gets a no-cooling call at 9 PM. Here is the connected journey these systems are designed to run.

  1. The AI Front Desk answers on the first ring. (Live today.)
  2. Service area and urgency are checked against the company's approved rules. (Live today.)
  3. An appointment is booked or the call escalates to the on-call tech. (Live today.)
  4. The customer record updates automatically with the call summary. (Planned.)
  5. The customer gets confirmation by text. (Live today.)
  6. The owner gets the summary. (Live today.)
  7. The open estimate gets approved follow-up until the customer decides. (Planned.)
  8. The completed job triggers a feedback request, and happy customers are invited to review. (Planned.)
  9. A maintenance reminder becomes eligible next season. (Planned.)
  10. Collected revenue is linked back to that original 9 PM call. (Planned.)

Straight answers

Coming Soon questions.

Are these services available now?

No. Only the AI Front Desk is available today. Everything else on this page is planned or being researched, and may change as we test with real businesses.

Can I start with just the AI Front Desk?

Yes, that is the intended starting point. It works on its own, and future services are designed to build on it.

Will I be able to buy one service without the whole stack?

That is the plan. Services are designed to be modular, so you add what your business actually needs.

Will these work with my current software?

Compatibility is confirmed per business before anything is promised. We never claim an integration exists until it is implemented and tested.

Will AI send messages or change records without approval?

No. Sensitive actions like pricing, complaints, and unusual replies are designed to require human approval, and message templates are approved by you first.

How will my customer information be protected?

Data is used only to run the service you configure, with retention and access rules agreed before launch. See our privacy page for how this website itself behaves.

Will this replace my employees?

The goal is to remove repetitive phone and office work, not people. Humans stay in charge of judgment calls, pricing, and anything unusual.

Can I get into an early pilot?

Maybe. Use the interest form and tell us your biggest problem. If your business fits an early test, we will contact you directly.

Does submitting interest lock me into anything?

No. It is feedback that shapes what we build, nothing more.

How will future pricing work?

Future pricing is not set. Each service will get clear pricing before it launches, the same way the current plans are published.

Can I suggest a different workflow?

Yes, please do. The form has a free-text field for exactly that, and the best suggestions shape the roadmap.

How will Nevamis know a service is working?

Each service has a defined success metric before it launches, like response time for follow-up or recovered revenue for quotes, measured against your baseline.
