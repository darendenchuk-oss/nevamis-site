About Nevamis

Voicemail loses jobs. We got rid of it.

Nevamis exists because of a simple, expensive pattern: a customer calls a service business, nobody can pick up, the call hits voicemail, and the customer dials the next company on the list. The job is gone in ninety seconds, and the business never even knows it happened.

We build AI receptionists that end that pattern. Not a chatbot bolted onto a website: a voice agent on your real phone line that answers the way a sharp front-desk person would, qualifies the caller, books the work, and tells you exactly what happened.

The service is done for you. We build the agent around your business, test it against your real call scenarios, run a controlled pilot on your line, and then keep reviewing calls and tuning it. You never touch the technology.

Nevamis is built in Edmonton and serves Canadian businesses. It is a new studio, and deliberately small: we take on a limited number of builds at a time and stay close to every one.
