## Formulas
- Pilot acquisition cost = founder hours × hourly value + platform usage + paid acquisition
- Pilot→paid CAC = Σ pilot costs ÷ converted clients
- Setup payback = (CAC + unrecovered onboarding cost) ÷ monthly gross profit
- Monthly pilot capacity = available founder onboarding hours ÷ avg hours per pilot
