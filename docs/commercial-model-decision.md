## Pick exactly one

**Model A — Paid setup + 7-day risk-free launch (recommended by the brief)**
Setup fee paid before build; subscription confirmed before go-live; cancel in
the first 7 live days → first monthly fee refunded; setup fee earned once
build begins. Public language: "7-day risk-free launch." NOT called free.
Why recommended: protects founder onboarding hours (4.5–8.5h per build),
filters non-serious buyers, still gives real risk reversal.

**Model B — True free live pilot**
Qualified businesses get a tightly limited 7-day live pilot (1 line, 1 flow,
1 calendar, 60 min / 30 calls cap, one revision). No card, no automatic
billing; conversion requires explicit acceptance + setup fee + first month.
Cost: founder time + usage as deliberate acquisition spend. Public language:
"7-day live pilot · no card, no automatic billing."

**Model C — Credited onboarding deposit**
Deposit before pilot, credited in full to the setup fee on conversion; 7 live
days without subscription charge. Not called free.
